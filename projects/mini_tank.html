GENERAL INFORMATION:

Project Name: Mini-Tank
Project Status: In planning


DESCRIPTION:

	We want to build a man-powered paintball-armored-vehicle
(PAV).  I got the idea from the Warpig Tank forums.  The tank will
have two bicycle wheels on the front and two support legs in the
back.  It will be moved like a glorified wheel-barrow.  The tank
will be built to fit two people:  a gunner in the front and a driver
in the back.
	Additional plans include having spots on the inside to hold
markers while you drive.  Another idea that I have been playing with
is making a 3-D cow head to mount on the front.  This would put some
weight in front of the wheels and make the tank look like a charging
bull.

Sketch of one possible plan.
