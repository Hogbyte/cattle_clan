GENERAL INFORMATION:

Project Name: The Cone
Project Status: Completed
Timeframe: March 5 - 13, 2004
Design: David
Build Team: David, Daniel and Brian


DESCRIPTION:

	This project is a modification of a current bunker.  "The Cone"
is a large tractor tire that is sitting on four pillars of stacked planter
tires with an upside-down cone from a hog feeder sitting on top of it.  If
a person wanted, he could crawl between the support pillars and stand up
inside of the cone.  As it is, the cone bunker is vulnerable.  There are
numerous gaps in it's support base.  So we decided to build a more solid
support out of wood.
