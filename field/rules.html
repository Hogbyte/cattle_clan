Field Rules

General
  • When not on the field, only shoot at the target. Do not shoot buildings, equipment, or livestock.
  • Always have the safety on when not on the field or at the shooting range.
  • Always remove the air source and hopper when working on your maker.
  • Chronograph your marker; velocity must be less than 285 fps.
On the Field
  • Always wear a paintball approved mask while on the field.
  • If you are close enough to a person that if you shot you could cause injury, use the bang rule (see below).
Elimination
  • You are eliminated if a paintball breaks on your body and leaves a quarter sized mark, even if you were shot by a teammate.
  • You are eliminated if you are sprayed by a grenade and there is a quarter sized mark.
  • Paintball spray does not count as an elimination.
  • A hit to the gun does not count as an elimination.
  • If the paintball does not break you are not out.
  • When you say you are "hit" or "out" you must leave the field, you cannot return to the field if you yelled "hit" but then noticed that the paintball did not break.
The "Bang Rule"

If you are close enough to your opponent that if you shot you could possibly hurt him/her (within 15 feet), say "bang" and he/she is eliminated. You are not out if you are "banged" by your own teammate.
