We wanted a place to paintball regularly, but our grove was not too exciting because the trees were planted in rows. In the summer of 2003 we started moving tires and scrap lumber into the grove to give more cover. We started small with a couple of bunkers in the middle. Eventually we had a large group of people who came to play so we added the towers to the field as well as the team bases.
