To Wabasso
1.  On East College Drive, head East about 12 miles and turn right onto 68, you will be 
    heading south towards Milroy.

2.  Follow this road through Milroy.  About one mile out of Milroy the road curves left,
    follow the curve, you will be heading East towards Wabasso. (About 14 miles from Milroy)

From Wabasso, 
1.  Head East 3 1/2 miles on 68 until you reach an S curve.

2.  Halfway through the first segment of the S curve take a left onto a gravel road 
    (Kenwood Avenue).

3.  Continue going North for 3 1/2 miles until you see a white house with evergreens around
    it on the left side of the road.

4.  If you reach a T intersection you went too far.  Do a U turn, and pull into the second
    farm site on the right.

Additional Info:
To help you recognize the farm site: from the road you will notice rows of round straw bales
west of an old red barn.  There is another farm across from ours on the right side of the road.
The large white Morton building is where we will set up.  Feel free to park on the south side of 
the building if there is room.  You can also park closer to the house.
